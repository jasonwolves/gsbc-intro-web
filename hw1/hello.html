Jason Scott is a graduate of Golden State Baptist College with a bachelors degree in Pastoral Theology. He grew up in Napa, California and now lives in Long Beach California serving as the Assistant Pastor and Treasurer of Gethsemane Baptist Church. Even though he grew up in church, it was at the age of five on August 13th, 2006 where he accepted Jesus Christ as his Savior. At the age of ten he was called to preach and from there his goal was set. He is a proud bus captain, and enjoys reading, playing ping-pong, and messing around with Photoshop and Coding in his free time.

I was blessed to grow up at the great Hopewell Baptist Church and have learned many things. It has grown far more than I ever dreamt possible thanks to the Lord and from the support of all those who have invested in this project.
